Welcome to Online Technik DE

What is online technik?

Online technik is where you can borrow helpful tools. Rental duration is per semester.

Available resources

  • USB Webcamera,
  • Studio headphone,
  • headset with microphone,
  • KRK Rokit 7(studio (sound)monitor) rented out,
  • Dremel 4000,
  • Zoom H1,
  • Zoom H5 (very limited!)
  • Zoom Q2n,
  • laptops (very limited!)

Application for laptop rental

Limited amount of laptops are avaliable for up to 10 month rental. For rental, you need to present your liability insurance that covers rental laptops. To apply, please fill in the form: link to the application form
